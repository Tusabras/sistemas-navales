ABOUT

We are a boutique yacht charter company based in the beautiful city of Barcelona offering luxury yacht rental, sales and charter management. We work directly with marina owners and yacht crew to provide a superior client experience from the initial super yacht search to the charter itself.

We combine the latest immersive technology with the guidance of our team of seasoned industry experts to select your ideal yacht and itinerary. Our goal is to serve clients on an individual basis exploiting our local knowledges and extensive resource network to create your bespoke charter.
